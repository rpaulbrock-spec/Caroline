All supplements taken every day including rest days unless noted. Timing is designed to work with meals and training for maximum benefit.
⚡ Creatine Monohydrate
5g daily
With pre-workout snack or morning drink — every day including rest days
Supports strength, energy and recovery. Takes 2–3 weeks to fully load.
☀️ Vitamin D3
1,000–2,000 IU daily
Training days: with post-workout shake. Rest days: with breakfast.
Fat-soluble — must be taken with food for absorption.
🐟 Omega-3 Fish Oil
1–2g EPA/DHA daily
Same timing as Vitamin D3 — with fatty food for best absorption
Anti-inflammatory, supports joint health and recovery.
✨ Collagen Protein
10–15g (half scoop)
Afternoon snack — alongside a Vitamin C-rich food
Berries at the same time act as a cofactor for collagen synthesis. Myprotein hydrolysed bovine Type I & III.
🌙 Magnesium Glycinate
300mg every evening
With cottage cheese at 9:00pm
Supports deep sleep, muscle recovery, and reduces next-day soreness.
